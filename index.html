My Story

Fall 2017 - Spring 2019

I work in my first paid role as a Software Engineer at Crownpeak.

Fall 2016 - Summer 2017

Turing proves to be one of the hardest challenges I've taken on.
I graduate.

Spring 2016

With some validation of doing well in my classes, I decide to make a go at programming as a career. I apply to Turing School of Software & Design. I graduate college.

Fall 2015

I decide to take the plunge and learn how to program. With a couple close friends in the gaming programming major to lean on, I start teaching myself C++. I enroll in an HTML&CSS course, a C++ course, and a PhotoShop course for my final semseter of college.

Fall 2014

I study abroad in Dublin, Ireland. I volunteer at the Web Summit. I hear talks from Peter Thiel and Drew Houston.

Spring 2013

I attend Vermont's annual Peak Pitch competition to pitch the prototype of Menti. My co-founder, Chapin Bryce, and I stay up most of the night practicing our presentation.

Winter 2013

I spin up a team of students, from different discplines to start a project called Menti. I spend most of winter break writing up an executive summary. I'll go on to serve as the Project Manager for the team, without even knowing what that is at the time.

Fall 2012

I Start at Champlain College in Burlington, Vermont. My head is filled with excitement at the prospect of being an entreprenuer one day. I major in International Business.
